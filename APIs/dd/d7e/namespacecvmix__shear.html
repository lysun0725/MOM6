123 ! !DESCRIPTION:
124 ! Initialization routine for shear (Richardson number-based) mixing. There are
125 ! currently two supported schemes - set \verb|mix_scheme = 'PP'| to use the
126 ! Pacanowski-Philander mixing scheme or set \verb|mix_scheme = 'KPP'| to use
127 ! the interior mixing scheme laid out in Large et al.
128 !\\
129 !\\
130 ! PP requires setting $\nu_0$ (\verb|PP_nu_zero| in this routine), $alpha$
131 ! (\verb|PP_alpha|), and $n$ (\verb|PP_exp|), and returns
132 ! \begin{eqnarray*}
133 ! \nu_{PP} & = & \frac{\nu_0}{(1+\alpha \textrm{Ri})^n} + \nu_b \\
134 ! \kappa_{PP} & = & \frac{\nu}{1+\alpha \textrm{Ri}} + \kappa_b
135 ! \end{eqnarray*}
136 ! Note that $\nu_b$ and $\kappa_b$ are 0 by default, with the assumption that
137 ! background diffusivities are computed in the \verb|cvmix_background| module
